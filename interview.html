The Interview

Some doors open only once

⬥

The address came by hand. No return address. No name. Just a time, a location, and two words at the bottom: Come alone.

The house is older than the street it sits on. The door opens before you knock. There is no one behind it.

He is in the chair by the window. Not quite looking at you. Still in the specific way that living things are not still.

You found it without trouble, I hope. The street numbering in this city has always been somewhat optimistic."

*He looks at you now. His eyes are the colour of deep water. He gestures to the chair across from him.*

Sit. Please."

*You sit. The room settles around you. The candles do not move in any wind you can feel.*

You have ten questions. I will answer them as honestly as I am able. That is not a small offer. I want you to understand that before you waste any of them."

*A pause.*

Begin when you are ready."
